# BK-147 — Out Of Scope

> Jira field: `customfield_10075` · [View in Jira](https://jira.upexgalaxy.com/browse/BK-147)

- Editing, reordering, adding, or removing content inside an opened item — those remain gated by their own stories (e.g. BK-28). Workbench tabs are read-and-navigate surfaces only.
- Any backend or data-model change. This is a presentation-layer reorganization over the existing views.
- Building the navigation destinations that do not yet exist — Home dashboard, ATC Library global index, Test Runs, Bug Reports, Metrics, Settings. They remain visible-but-disabled ("coming soon") entries, gated by the data domains that back them.
- Tabs that span more than one project.
- Persisting open tabs across sessions or devices.
